My Future Career Plans

After this semester, I am hoping to attend MUSC invasive cardiovascular technology school. This is an 18 month program that would allow me to work as a cardiovascular technologoist. I would be working in a cathederization lab at the hospital. This field is growing rapidly.

After I have worked for a few years as a CVT, I may choose to go back to school. If I go back to school, it will be to physicians assistant school. I eventually see myself working in an out-patient center.
